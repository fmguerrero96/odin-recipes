Steak Tacos

Dish Description

Steak tacos are one of Mexico's most popular dishes. They are suitable for any ocassion. Serve these delicious steak tacos to your friends and they will be delighted. If spicy foods aren't your favorite, then these tacos are perfect for you as they don't require any spicy ingredients. It is up to your taste to add a spicy salsa or hot sauce. Without further introduction, let's get into this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas of your choice
  • Fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 diced yellow onion
  • Fresh limes
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Diced steak of your choice (for best results, ribeye steak is recommended)
  • (Optional) salsa or hot sauce of your choice

Preparation

  1. Place diced steak in a large container. Add salt and pepper to taste and let rest for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Dice your onion and chop your cilantro very finely.
  3. Cut limes into quarters.
  4. Heat up cooking oil in a large skillet. Be careful not to burn the oil.
  5. Sear your steak until it is completely cooked through.
  6. On a separate skillet, warm up your tortillas untill they are soft and slightly chared.
  7. On a warm tortilla, place a generous serving of your steak and top with cilantro and onion.
  8. Squeeze some lime juice onto the taco and add salsa or hot sauce if you so desire.
